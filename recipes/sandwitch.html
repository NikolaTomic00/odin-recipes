Sandwitch

Description

This sandwich is quick, tasty, and perfect for lunch or a snack. It includes fresh vegetables, cheese, and your choice of protein, all layered between slices of bread

You can customize it with your favorite sauces and toppings for a perfect bite every time!

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cheese
  • ham
  • tomato

Steps

  1. Slice the bread
  2. Use ketchup
  3. Put ham and cheese
  4. Enjoy delicious sandwitch
